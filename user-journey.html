🟢 Automated by the System

  • Account selection: Picks top-scored unworked account from ranked hopper.
  • Research: Refreshes account intel (triggers, sub-orgs, pain points).
  • Contact sourcing: Finds VP/Director+ ICP contacts in Clay, enriches verified emails.
  • Content writing: Generates bespoke email, call script, LinkedIn video, and connection note per contact.
  • Apollo staging: Creates contacts, populates custom fields, stages sequence.
  • Reply monitoring: Polls Gmail every 15 min, classifies, scores real replies.

🟡 Human Touch Points

  • STRIKE review: Read and approve personalized content for top-tier logos.
  • Enroll confirm: Slack reply "enroll [account]" to unlock sending.
  • Import CSV: One-time Apollo UI import to map per-contact custom fields.
  • Activate: Unpause sequence in Apollo to start the cadence.
  • Calls & video: Execute call openers and LinkedIn videos yourself.
  • Hot handoff: Follow up on scored replies the system surfaces.
